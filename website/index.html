About

QuietIoT is an IoT-powered noise monitoring system for any work environment. It is designed to give users quick and clear insights into the noise in different parts of their offices, homes or study areas. By doing so, QuietIoT aims to help reduce distractions from noise and improve productivity.

How to Use

Place the IoT devices in different areas of the workspace you want to monitor (e.g. different rooms). These devices contain a microphone to measure noise levels and a screen to display quick readings. The IoT devices connect over Wi-Fi to the internet and send data to the cloud. This data can then be used to display a variety of charts found in the dashboard.
